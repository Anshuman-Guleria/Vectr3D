# 🚀 VECTR3D

**A browser-based 2D → 3D Extrusion Engine**

Transform images or hand-drawn shapes into interactive 3D models instantly — directly in the browser.

**No Blender. No installations. Just upload, draw, and extrude.**

---

## ✨ Features

✅ **Upload PNG/SVG** → Automatically traces edges and converts them into 3D models

✅ **Draw Mode** → Sketch any shape and watch it turn into 3D live

✅ **Material Presets**

* Matte
* Metallic
* Glass
* Neon Glow

✅ **Customization Controls**

* Extrusion depth
* Bevel edges
* Custom colors

✅ **Export Support**

* Save final 3D renders as PNG

---

## 🧠 How It Works

VECTR3D detects contours from uploaded images or drawings, converts them into geometry, and extrudes them into customizable 3D meshes in real time.

One of the biggest challenges while building this project was creating a **clean contour tracing system** that could reliably detect edges from real-world images with irregular boundaries and convert them into usable 3D geometry.

---

## 🔥 Why VECTR3D?

Most 3D modeling workflows require heavy software, installations, or technical experience.

VECTR3D simplifies the process by allowing users to **turn 2D shapes into interactive 3D models instantly in the browser**, making experimentation faster and more accessible.
